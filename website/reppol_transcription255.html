Edmond Schethers answer ot the 13 Articles

1

Dr Willoughby

To the first : I know not the begynners of thes matters at london but I think that Mr serles talked with doctor london of them as he cam from canter to oxxon And I think also that Mr Williby moved such matters much ther by reson he said that he had spoken with many of the kinges cownsell in it & that he wold take upon him to be the hole setter forth of the matter. After the woordes Mr baker did send for Mr parkhurst Mr gardener & me.

2

I know none that gave cownsell ther but I think that Mr williby did much of his own hedde

3

Mr Baker wintomen D. london

I know not who cousend ther in the matter other wise then as I have said that Mr baker shewd us the book the which my lord of wynchester rede & doctor london took the names of the witnes of the same

4

I know no grownd thei hadd but I think that the coming of Sir turner & Sir anthony parson & other owt of other dioces in to this: And the resort that was so fare of : to Sir turners sermons: And the subscribing of Sir bland to his articles as it is said: And the reding of sur anthony parson of the bible & other with adding ther expositions to the same was a gret occasion of it

5

I know not who declared ony grownd ther but I think Mr williby shewd the book & moved the matter much ther

n 6

I know no matters ony in london knoweth my lordes grace culpable in but in such articles as master baker willed us to say what we hard we did

London Gardiner

1

I know not the begynner of it in canter but I suspect Mr coxston & Mr gardener by reyso that coxston did use to write all such matters And many articles of the book wer known only to Mr gardener. As for other matters the fame was grete

2

Gardiner willoughby

I know none that the promoter but Mr gardener & williby as towching the books before such tyme that it was subscribed then the witnes wer all of cownsell that Mr williby hadd the book

3

Mr gardener & williby conferd to gither befor Mr williby receved the book & after such as subscribe talked of the articles sometyme & of other articles as we hard them spoken

4

I know no grownd but I thinke as I have said in the 4 article above and also bicause the prorses aganst Mr rydley & scory went not forth as against Mr serles & me: Also bycause Joan barnes, gilles barran, bukner (?) & other of when much talking was of, wer not poonisshed & other matters which I remember not that men said wer much borne

5

I do not know what knowledge other men hadd

n

6

I have hard men say many thinges as that my lord did nkow of bukners opynion of Joan barnes opynion: that gilles of barran used the office of a prest being no prest & other thinges which were not reformed but thes thinges manye wer done before I cam in to thes partes & whrin he my lordes grace is culpable it is not in me to say nor I know not what other do know my lord to be culpable in
